Whitening FAQs

Does laser teeth whitening cause pain?
No, laser teeth whitening at SLVR Dental is pain-free. We apply a protective barrier on your gums and use desensitizing elements, ensuring a comfortable experience.
How long do teeth whitening results last?
Typically, results last 1 to 2 years. Proper oral hygiene and avoiding smoking or staining foods (like tea/coffee) will keep your teeth white for much longer.

Root Canal FAQs

Is a Root Canal painful?
With modern anesthesia and rotary endodontic tools used by Dr. Vishnu, a Root Canal feels no different from a regular tooth filling. It relieves the tooth pain immediately.
How many visits are required for RCT?
We specialize in Single-Visit Root Canals which take only 45-60 minutes. In cases of severe chronic infection, it may require a second visit to ensure absolute sterility.

Implant FAQs

Who is eligible for dental implants?
Any adult with missing teeth who has adequate jawbone density and healthy gums is a candidate. If bone density is low, bone grafting options can be discussed.
What is the recovery time?
Post-surgery soreness lasts only a few days. The bonding phase (implant integrating with jawbone) takes 3 to 6 months before the final crown is placed. Temporary crowns are provided.

Makeover FAQs

What treatments are included in a Smile Makeover?
Depending on your needs, a smile makeover can combine teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, cosmetic bonding, gum contouring, and orthodontic aligners.
How long does a Smile Makeover take?
Porcelain veneers and bonding take only 1 to 2 weeks (2 visits). If alignment therapy with braces or clear aligners is needed, it can take 6 to 12 months.
