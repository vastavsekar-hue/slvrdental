A Legacy of Trusted Dental Excellence

Established in 2017, SLVR Dental and Cosmetic Care was founded with a clear vision: to bring world-class, advanced dental treatments to Tirupati at ethical and transparent pricing.

Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted dental clinics in the region. We combine the expertise of qualified specialists with cutting-edge medical technologies (such as digital rotary systems and ultra-low radiation X-rays) to ensure every treatment is highly precise, successful, and entirely pain-free.

We understand that visiting the dentist can cause anxiety. That is why our clinic layout is designed to feel warm and relaxing, and our team is highly trained in gentle dental phobia management.
