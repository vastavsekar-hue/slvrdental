Discreet Teeth Straightening for Adults & Teens

Crooked, crowded, or spaced teeth can impact your self-esteem and lead to chewing issues, speech difficulties, and increased plaque accumulation. Traditional metal braces are effective but visible and can restrict the foods you eat.

At SLVR Dental and Cosmetic Care, we offer modern **Clear Aligners in Tirupati** as a comfortable, invisible alternative. Using digital 3D intraoral scans, we map your entire alignment journey. Your teeth are shifted gradually using custom-designed, medical-grade polyurethane trays that can be removed for eating and cleaning.

Why Opt for Clear Aligners?

✓ Virtually Invisible: Straighten your teeth without anyone noticing.
✓ Removable Trays: Eat your favorite foods and maintain excellent oral hygiene.
✓ Comfortable Fit: Smooth edges with zero painful metal wires or brackets.
✓ Affordable EMI Plans: Flexible monthly payment structures.

Aligners FAQs

How much do clear aligners cost in Tirupati?
Clear aligners are customized based on the complexity of teeth shifting and duration of treatment. We offer flexible EMI plans to make clear aligners highly affordable. Contact us at +91 73823 02125 for a free 3D digital dental scan and precise quotation.
Can I get clear aligners instead of traditional braces?
Yes, in most cases. Clear aligners can treat mild to complex orthodontic cases like overcrowding, gaps, and overbites. Severe skeletal bite discrepancies might still require ceramic or metal braces. An orthodontic checkup will determine your ideal treatment.
How many hours a day do I need to wear clear aligners?
To achieve successful alignment according to schedule, you must wear your aligner trays for 20 to 22 hours per day. You should only remove them while eating, drinking hot liquids, brushing, and flossing.
How long does the aligners treatment take?
On average, active aligners treatment takes 6 to 12 months, which is often faster than traditional metal braces. Simple tooth gaps can be resolved in as little as 4 to 6 months.
