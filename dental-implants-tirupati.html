Permanent Tooth Replacement

State-of-the-Art Tooth Implants under Expert Care

Missing teeth can affect your speech, restrict what you eat, and lead to jawbone shrinking over time. Standard bridges require shaving down adjacent healthy teeth. Dental implants are the gold standard because they replace the tooth root directly.

At SLVR Dental and Cosmetic Care, Chief Dentist Dr. Vishnu Kumar utilizes advanced digital 3D CBCT scans to plan surgical guides, placing Swiss and US biocompatible titanium implants (Straumann, Nobel Biocare) with extreme precision. The resulting tooth looks, feels, and functions exactly like a natural tooth.

Key Benefits of Dental Implants

✓ Lifetime Durability: Titanium post fuses naturally with jawbone.
✓ Preserves Jawbone Structure: Prevents bone loss & facial sagging.
✓ No Damage to Adjacent Teeth: Unlike conventional bridges.
✓ Natural Aesthetics: High-end CAD/CAM zirconia crown matches natural shade.

Your Implant Journey

1

3D Scan & Surgical Guide Planning

We analyze your jawbone thickness and nerve channels using a 3D digital dental scanner to determine precise angle placement.

2

Painless Implant Placement

Under local anesthesia, the medical-grade titanium post is placed into the bone. The procedure takes under 45 minutes and is entirely pain-free.

3

Osseointegration & Zirconia Crown

As the bone heals and bonds to the implant (taking 3-6 months), we install a custom crown, restoring 100% biting capability.

Implant FAQs

What is the cost of dental implants in Tirupati?
The cost of dental implants varies based on the implant system chosen (Indian, German, Swiss like Straumann, or US like Nobel Biocare) and patient-specific jawbone density. We offer very competitive, transparent prices and flexible EMI payment options. Contact us at +91 73823 02125 for a personalized checkup and estimate.
Are dental implants painful?
No. The procedure is performed under complete local anesthesia, meaning you will feel no pain during the surgery. After the numbing wears off, any mild soreness can be easily managed with standard prescribed painkillers.
How long does the dental implant procedure take?
The surgical placement of the titanium post takes only about 30 to 45 minutes per implant. After placement, a healing period of 3 to 6 months is required for the implant to bond with your jawbone (osseointegration) before attaching the final zirconia crown.
What is the success rate of dental implants at SLVR Dental?
Under our expert Implantologist Dr. Vishnu Kumar, our clinic maintains a success rate of over 98%. We achieve this through meticulous 3D digital diagnostic planning and utilizing premium, medical-grade bio-compatible titanium implants.
