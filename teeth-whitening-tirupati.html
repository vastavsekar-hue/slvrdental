Enamel-Safe, Professional Teeth Bleaching

Stained or discolored teeth from drinking coffee, tea, smoking, or natural aging can make you feel self-conscious. While over-the-counter charcoal pastes or whitening strips promise results, they are often abrasive and damage your enamel, causing severe tooth sensitivity.

At SLVR Dental and Cosmetic Care, we offer professional **Laser Teeth Whitening in Tirupati** that is completely safe and fast. Dr. Vishnu Kumar uses premium, neutral-pH whitening gels and targets them with a specialized dental laser. This lifts stubborn internal stains deep in the enamel without eroding the tooth structure.

Treatment Steps

1

Shade Analysis & Scaling

A specialized laser light is positioned to activate the gel. In just 45 minutes, deep yellow pigments are broken down, leaving you with a bright smile.

Teeth Whitening FAQs

Does laser teeth whitening cause pain or damage enamel?
No. At SLVR Dental, we use professional, enamel-safe bleaching gels activated by soft laser light. We apply a protective barrier on your gums and use desensitizing elements, making the session entirely safe, pain-free, and comfortable.
How long do teeth whitening results last?
Results typically last between 1 to 2 years. This depends on your daily habits—avoiding heavily staining food, tobacco, coffee, and maintaining proper brushing and scaling visits will keep your smile bright much longer.
How much does professional teeth whitening cost in Tirupati?
Professional whitening is a highly cost-effective cosmetic procedure. The price depends on whether you opt for in-office laser whitening (fastest, most dramatic results) or a custom take-home whitening kit. Call us at +91 73823 02125 for a transparent quote.
How long does a laser teeth whitening session take?
An in-office laser whitening session is very fast, taking only about 45 minutes to complete. You will walk out of our clinic with a visibly brighter smile immediately!
